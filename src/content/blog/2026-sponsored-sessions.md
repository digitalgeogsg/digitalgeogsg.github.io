This is a running list of sessions that the DGSG will be sponsoring for the upcoming AAG in San Francisco. For more information on session sponsorship, [please see our previous post](https://digitalgeogsg.github.io/posts/2025-see-you-in-detroit/). We will be adding links to the sessions as they become available in the AAG's digital program.

Join the Digital Geographies Specialty Group at AAG 2026 for our Business Meeting on Wednesday, March 18 (11:40 AM – 12:40 PM, Nob Hill 10) and our Digital Geographies Social from 2:30 – 3:50 PM (Union Square 15 & 16) — we’d love to see you there.
